Triangulum

Description

The Triangulum Galaxy, 3 million light-years away, gleams as the third-largest in the Local Group. A spiral beauty adorned with clusters and nebulae, it hosts a stellar nursery, nurturing new stars in its vibrant arms. Its allure lies in distant skies, a cosmic spectacle for avid stargazers and astronomers.
