Pinwheel Galaxy

Description

The Pinwheel Galaxy, 21 million light-years away, enchants with its grand spiral arms swirling in cosmic harmony. Known as Messier 101, it hosts vibrant clusters and glowing nebulae, a celestial canvas painting a tapestry of stellar birth and evolution. Its resplendence beckons astronomers, unveiling the secrets of distant galaxies.
