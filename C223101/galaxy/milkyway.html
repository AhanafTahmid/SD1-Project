Milky Way

Description

The Milky Way, our cosmic home, is a spiral galaxy spanning 100,000 light-years. It's a tapestry of billions of stars, gas, and dust, with a luminous core harboring a supermassive black hole. Earth resides in one of its spiral arms, amidst celestial wonders that inspire awe and curiosity in astronomers and dreamers alike.
